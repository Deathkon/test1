ABOUT US

Havity Group is a software company we are behind on innovative solutions which bring impact to community like Havity Pay and Havity Health.

Our Story

Our solutions empowers businesses and individuals, bring impact to lives and making life simpler, more secure, and full of possibilities.

The Havity Team is a diverse and well-rounded group of co-founders, each bringing unique expertise to the table. Benilde is a highly creative and customer-centric designer with a strong business acumen. Philbert is an experienced entrepreneur, while Nelson specializes in software engineering and cybersecurity with a background in the banking sector. Oscar is a driven software engineer and developer who constantly pushes boundaries. The team's formation was influenced by college connections and existing friendships, fostering a strong and collaborative working environment. At Havity Group, we believe in promoting gender equality, and our company is female-led, exemplifying our commitment to diversity and inclusion. Together, our team combines business acumen, technical expertise, and creativity to drive innovation and develop cutting-edge solutions. We are united by a shared vision of driving financial inclusion through secure and user-friendly payment solutions, empowering businesses and consumers in developing countries. As a cohesive team, we are dedicated to making a positive impact in the digital payment industry and beyond. {% comment %}
